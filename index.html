Server for everyone

Store and backup all files using smartphone or computer. Blox has different folders for guest and owners to avoid deletion and unwanted copying of files.

Owners

Full Access

Owners has there own accounts and private folders. they can also create new accounts for their family and friends.

Guests

Limited Access

Guests can only store and download files on public folders and external drives plugged on Blox. Guests need a username and password to access other folders such as eg: Videos, Pictures, Musics etc.

Media Streaming

Simuoltaneously play and view videos, music and pictures with your friends and family using smartphone or computer.

Stream Anywhere

Anyone connected to Blox can watch & play stored videos, music and pictures using browser or app.

Blox Browser App

Let's you control and look whats inside the Blox. With it's custom preferences you can put your personal touch to the app.

No installation needed just go to browser and type: blox.home/

Interactive Entertainment System

Let's you control entertainment devices using smartphone or computer.

Single Device

Shutdown

Let's you shutdown or restart your Blox even your not in your home.

Control

Use your smartphone or tablet as mouse and keyboard.

Smart TV

Blox will make your normal TV become a smart TV.

Multiple Device

Note: You need a Blox mini to control 2 or more entertainment devices.
